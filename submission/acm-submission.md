# GhostVeil Oracle Swarm ACM Submission

GhostVeil Oracle Swarm is a Solana alpha verification agent built for traders who want early opportunities without drowning in noise.

The agent scans wallet movement, liquidity changes, launch activity, social momentum, and narrative shifts. Instead of publishing every signal, GhostVeil sends candidates through a multi-agent Alpha Tribunal where bull, bear, risk, liquidity, and timing agents debate the opportunity. Weak, risky, manipulated, or crowded signals are rejected.

Signals that survive become GhostVeil Alpha Cards with:

- Stealth Score
- Conviction Score
- Risk Score
- Evidence trail
- Alpha Tribunal summary
- GhostTrade Replay
- Trade risk warnings
- Invalidation points
- X/Discord-ready summary

VeilGuard protects trader intent by separating private analysis from public output. Public cards keep the useful evidence while hiding sensitive wallet intent, trade size assumptions, and private strategy details.

GhostVeil is monetized through Premium Pro Alpha Cards at $1 per high-value card. 40% of Premium Signal revenue funds GhostBack Rewards for Signal Scouts, Alpha Validators, Card Distributors, and Power Users.

The product is designed for Swarms Marketplace distribution, x402 payments, and Frenzy Mode tokenization.
